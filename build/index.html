Ehvam

International Spiritual Center for Peace

Ehvam is envisioned as a regional and international advanced spiritual center for peace. It will take form on a unique desert hill top, near the ancient city of Moa in the Arava Region, on the path of the historic Incense Route. Its location is close to the Jordanian and Israeli Border, at the heart of one of the most disruptive conflict zones in the world – the Middle East.

At Ehvam, students from the region and from around the world, will study and practice the principles of morality, compassion, generosity, wisdom, and the science of inner transformation, all based in authentic wisdom traditions of the world. The center will welcome and be open to the study and practice of authentic spiritual traditions that teach methods to reduce suffering and increase harmony and peace in our world.

Our Inspiration

Soon after she started teaching in Israel, Lama Dvora established the School of Heart-Dharma. Over the years more than a hundred teachers were trained by her, who now teach in groups all over the country. This momentum led to the establishment of the Arava Spiritual Center, a precursor of Ehvam, located approximately two miles from the proposed permanent site of Ehvam.

Lama Dvora

Lama Dvora is the founder and spiritual director of the Arava Spiritual Center in Israel. She holds M.Sc. and PH.D. degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science, and has worked in academia, as well as research and financial institutions.

About Ehvam

The name Ehvam is a combination of two Sanskrit syllables and has a deep symbolic meaning in the Buddhist teaching.
Our Vision

Ehvam is a pioneering international spiritual & retreat center, located in the midst of the conflict-torn Middle East.

We believe that a significant and lasting change in the world, must begin with a deep inner change within the individual’s heart and mind.
